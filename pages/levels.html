Levels

Levels are the different stages of the game. Each level has its own unique challenges and objectives.

As you progress through the levels, the difficulty increases, and new elements are introduced.

Here are some examples of levels:

  • Level 1: Introduction to the game mechanics.
  • Level 2: Basic challenges with limited resources.
  • Level 3: Advanced challenges with new elements.
  • Level 4: Expert challenges that require strategic thinking.
  • Level 5: Final level with the ultimate challenge.

Each level is designed to test your skills and keep you engaged. Good luck!
